Module 2: Consumer Psychology

Topic 3: Emotion in Marketing

Emotion plays a powerful and often subconscious role in consumer decision-making. Marketers leverage emotions to create connections, build brand loyalty, and drive purchases.

How Emotions Influence Marketing:

  • Brand Connection: Emotional advertising can create strong bonds between consumers and brands, leading to increased loyalty.
  • Memory and Recall: Emotionally charged experiences and advertisements are more memorable.
  • Impulse Purchases: Many purchasing decisions, especially for lower-priced items, are driven by immediate emotional responses.
  • Trust and Credibility: Positive emotions like joy, trust, and security can enhance a brand's perceived credibility.

Understanding which emotions resonate with your target audience and how to evoke them ethically is a key skill for any marketer.
