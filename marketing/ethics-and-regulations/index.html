Module 12: Ethics and Regulations

Introduction

Welcome to the Ethics and Regulations module! In this final module, we will explore the critical importance of ethical considerations and legal compliance in the world of marketing.

As marketers, we have a responsibility to our consumers, our companies, and society at large. Understanding and adhering to ethical principles and regulations is not just about avoiding penalties; it's about building trust and long-term brand reputation.

What You Will Learn:

  • Key data privacy laws and their impact on marketing practices.
  • The principles of ethical marketing and responsible advertising.
  • Distinguishing between genuine sustainability efforts and "greenwashing."

Let's market responsibly!
