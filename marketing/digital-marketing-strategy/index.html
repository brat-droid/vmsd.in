Module 6: Digital Marketing Strategy

Introduction

Welcome to the Digital Marketing Strategy module! In today's interconnected world, a robust digital presence is non-negotiable for businesses. This module will guide you through crafting effective digital marketing strategies.

Digital marketing encompasses all marketing efforts that use an electronic device or the internet. Businesses leverage digital channels such as search engines, social media, email, and other websites to connect with current and prospective customers.

What You Will Learn:

  • The fundamentals of Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Search Engine Marketing (SEM).
  • How to develop a compelling social media strategy.
  • Best practices for email marketing campaigns.
  • Understanding and building effective marketing funnels.

Let's master the digital landscape!
