Module 6: Digital Marketing Strategy

Topic 1: SEO/SEM

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Search Engine Marketing (SEM) are crucial components of digital marketing, focusing on increasing visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs).

Search Engine Optimization (SEO):

  • Definition: The practice of increasing the quantity and quality of traffic to your website through organic search engine results.
  • Key Aspects:
    • Keyword Research: Identifying terms and phrases people use to search for information.
    • On-page SEO: Optimizing website content, titles, meta descriptions, and internal linking.
    • Off-page SEO: Building high-quality backlinks from other reputable websites.
    • Technical SEO: Ensuring website crawlability, mobile-friendliness, and site speed.
  • Goal: To rank higher in unpaid search results, driving free, qualified traffic.

Search Engine Marketing (SEM):

  • Definition: A broader term that includes SEO, but primarily refers to paid search advertising (e.g., Google Ads, Bing Ads).
  • Key Aspects:
    • Pay-Per-Click (PPC): Advertisers pay a fee each time their ad is clicked.
    • Ad Copywriting: Crafting compelling ad text that encourages clicks.
    • Landing Page Optimization: Ensuring the page users land on after clicking the ad is relevant and converts effectively.
    • Bid Management: Strategically managing bids for keywords to maximize ROI.
  • Goal: To gain immediate visibility and drive targeted traffic through paid advertisements.

Both SEO and SEM are vital for a comprehensive digital marketing strategy, offering different benefits for short-term and long-term visibility.
