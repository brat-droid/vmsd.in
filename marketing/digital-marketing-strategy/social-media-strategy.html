Module 6: Digital Marketing Strategy

Topic 2: Social Media Strategy

A social media strategy outlines how your organization will use social media to achieve its communication and marketing goals. It's not just about posting; it's about engaging, listening, and building community.

Key Steps to Develop a Social Media Strategy:

  • Define Your Goals: What do you want to achieve? (e.g., brand awareness, lead generation, customer service, sales).
  • Identify Your Target Audience: Which platforms do they use? What content do they engage with?
  • Choose Your Platforms: Focus on platforms where your target audience is most active and where your content can thrive.
  • Content Strategy: Plan the types of content you'll create (e.g., videos, images, articles, stories), your posting frequency, and content themes.
  • Engagement Strategy: How will you interact with your audience? (e.g., respond to comments, run polls, host Q&A sessions).
  • Measurement & Analysis: Track key metrics (e.g., reach, engagement, conversions) to assess performance and make adjustments.
  • Competitor Analysis: Learn from what your competitors are doing well and where they are falling short.

A well-crafted social media strategy is dynamic and should be regularly reviewed and adapted based on performance and evolving trends.
