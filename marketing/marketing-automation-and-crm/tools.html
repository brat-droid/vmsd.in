Module 11: Marketing Automation & CRM

Topic 1: Tools (Hubspot, Mailchimp, Salesforce)

The right tools are essential for implementing effective marketing automation and CRM strategies. Here's an overview of some popular platforms:

HubSpot:

  • Type: All-in-one inbound marketing, sales, and service platform.
  • Features: CRM, marketing automation (email, workflows, lead scoring), content management (blogging, landing pages), sales tools, customer service tools, analytics.
  • Strengths: Comprehensive suite, user-friendly interface, strong focus on inbound methodology.

Mailchimp:

  • Type: Email marketing and marketing automation platform.
  • Features: Email campaign creation, audience segmentation, automation workflows, landing page builder, basic CRM functionalities, reporting.
  • Strengths: Excellent for email marketing, intuitive drag-and-drop editor, good for small to medium businesses.

Salesforce:

  • Type: Leading cloud-based CRM platform with extensive marketing capabilities (through Salesforce Marketing Cloud).
  • Features: Sales automation, customer service, marketing automation, analytics, app development. Highly customizable and scalable.
  • Strengths: Industry leader, highly robust and scalable, extensive ecosystem of integrations, powerful for large enterprises.

Choosing the right tool depends on your business size, specific needs, budget, and desired level of integration across marketing, sales, and service functions.
