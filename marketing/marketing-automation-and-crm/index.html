Module 11: Marketing Automation & CRM

Introduction

Welcome to the Marketing Automation & CRM module! In this module, we will explore how technology can streamline marketing efforts, personalize customer interactions, and manage customer relationships more effectively.

Marketing automation refers to software that automates repetitive marketing tasks. Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems help manage and analyze customer interactions and data throughout the customer lifecycle.

What You Will Learn:

  • An overview of popular marketing automation and CRM tools.
  • Strategies for effective lead nurturing.
  • How to map and optimize the customer journey.

Let's automate and optimize customer relationships!
