Module 4: Market Research and Segmentation

Introduction

Welcome to the Market Research and Segmentation module! In this module, we will learn how to gather crucial information about your target market and divide it into distinct groups to better serve their needs.

Effective market research provides the insights necessary to make informed marketing decisions, while segmentation allows for more targeted and efficient marketing efforts.

What You Will Learn:

  • The differences and applications of primary and secondary market research.
  • How to create detailed buyer personas to represent your ideal customers.
  • The process of Segmentation, Targeting, and Positioning (STP) as a strategic marketing framework.

Let's uncover market insights!
