Module 10: Growth Hacking & Virality

Introduction

Welcome to the Growth Hacking & Virality module! In this module, we will explore innovative and often unconventional strategies to achieve rapid business growth and make products or ideas spread like wildfire.

Growth hacking is a marketing technique that uses creativity, analytical thinking, and social metrics to sell products and gain exposure. It is often associated with startups, but can be applied to any business looking for rapid growth.

What You Will Learn:

  • The concept of viral loops and how to design them.
  • Strategies for implementing effective referral systems.
  • Analyzing successful growth hacking tactics through real-world case studies.

Let's unlock exponential growth!
