Module 3: Branding and Positioning

Introduction

Welcome to the Branding and Positioning module! In this module, we will explore how businesses create a unique identity and carve out a distinct space in the minds of their target audience.

Branding is more than just a logo; it's the entire experience and perception consumers have of a company or product. Positioning is about how you differentiate your offering from competitors.

What You Will Learn:

  • The fundamentals of developing a strong brand strategy.
  • How to use positioning maps to identify market opportunities.
  • Crafting a compelling value proposition that resonates with customers.
  • Analyzing successful branding and positioning through real-world case studies.

Let's build powerful brands!
