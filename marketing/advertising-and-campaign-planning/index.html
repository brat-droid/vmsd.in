Module 7: Advertising & Campaign Planning

Introduction

Welcome to the Advertising & Campaign Planning module! In this module, you will learn the art and science of creating compelling advertisements and orchestrating successful marketing campaigns.

Effective advertising is about more than just catchy slogans; it's about strategic communication designed to persuade and influence your target audience.

What You Will Learn:

  • The principles of effective ad copywriting.
  • How to plan and execute media buys for your campaigns.
  • Understanding the full lifecycle of a marketing campaign.
  • The importance and methodology of A/B testing for optimization.

Let's craft impactful campaigns!
