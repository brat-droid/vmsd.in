Module 8: Analytics and Performance

Topic 1: Google Analytics

Google Analytics is a web analytics service offered by Google that tracks and reports website traffic. It's an indispensable tool for marketers to understand how users interact with their websites, providing insights that can drive strategic decisions.

Key Features and Reports in Google Analytics:

  • Audience Reports: Provide insights into who your users are (demographics, interests, geography, technology used).
  • Acquisition Reports: Show how users are arriving at your website (e.g., organic search, social media, direct, referral, paid ads).
  • Behavior Reports: Reveal what users do once they are on your site (e.g., pages visited, time on page, bounce rate, site content, site speed).
  • Conversion Reports: Track goal completions and e-commerce transactions, helping you measure the effectiveness of your marketing efforts.
  • Real-time Reports: Show current activity on your site, including the number of active users, top pages, and traffic sources.
  • Custom Reports & Dashboards: Allow you to create personalized reports focusing on the metrics most important to your business.

By regularly analyzing Google Analytics data, marketers can identify trends, optimize website performance, improve user experience, and make data-driven decisions to achieve their marketing objectives.
