Module 8: Analytics and Performance

Introduction

Welcome to the Analytics and Performance module! In today's data-driven marketing landscape, measuring and analyzing performance is crucial for optimizing campaigns and demonstrating ROI.

This module will equip you with the knowledge and tools to effectively track, analyze, and improve your marketing efforts.

What You Will Learn:

  • How to use Google Analytics to gain insights into website traffic and user behavior.
  • Understanding Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and calculating Return on Investment (ROI).
  • Strategies for Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) to maximize your marketing effectiveness.

Let's dive into the numbers!
