Design

Good design is about more than just aesthetics; it's about creating a user-friendly and effective experience. This article covers the fundamental principles of design that will help you create visually appealing and intuitive websites.

Core Principles

The principles of design are the rules that govern how to combine elements to create a cohesive and effective composition. This article will cover the basics of contrast, repetition, alignment, and proximity (CRAP), and how to use them to create a strong visual hierarchy.

Example: Contrast

/* High contrast for readability */
body {
  background-color: #000;
  color: #fff; /* High contrast white on black */
}

Color and Typography

Color and typography are two of the most important elements of design. This article will cover the basics of color theory and how to choose a color palette that is both aesthetically pleasing and accessible.
