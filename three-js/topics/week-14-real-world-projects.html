Applying Your Skills to Build Impressive 3D Web Applications

This final week is dedicated to bringing together all the knowledge and skills you've acquired throughout the course. You'll work on building complete, real-world 3D projects, showcasing your ability to create compelling and functional interactive experiences on the web.

Topics Covered:

  • Portfolio landing page with 3D - Design and implement an engaging 3D portfolio to showcase your work.
  • 3D product viewer - Build an interactive viewer for products, allowing users to explore them in 3D.
  • Small game or interactive scene - Develop a simple 3D game or an interactive narrative experience.
  • Architectural walkthrough - Create an immersive virtual tour of an architectural space.

What You Will Achieve This Week:

By the end of this week, you will have a portfolio of impressive 3D web projects that demonstrate your mastery of Three.js and React Three Fiber. You'll be confident in tackling complex 3D development challenges and ready to apply your skills to a wide range of real-world applications, from interactive websites to immersive games and simulations.
