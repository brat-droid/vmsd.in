Optimizing Your 3D Scenes for Smooth and Efficient Experiences

Performance is critical for delivering high-quality 3D web experiences. This week, you'll learn essential optimization techniques and best practices to ensure your React Three Fiber applications run smoothly across various devices and platforms, even with complex scenes.

Topics Covered:

  • Reduce draw calls & optimize assets - Learn strategies to minimize rendering overhead and improve frame rates.
  • Use Instances, useMemo, preload, lazy - Leverage R3F and React features for efficient rendering and asset management.
  • Device compatibility (mobile, VR) - Adapt your 3D applications to perform well on different hardware and form factors.

What You Will Achieve This Week:

By the end of this week, you will be equipped with advanced optimization techniques to ensure your R3F projects deliver exceptional performance. You'll understand how to identify and resolve performance bottlenecks, making your 3D web applications fast, responsive, and accessible to a wider audience.
