Typography

Welcome to the world of typography, the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and appealing when displayed. This module will take you on a deep dive into the principles and practices that govern effective typography, from the foundational anatomy of a letterform to the sophisticated art of pairing fonts and creating a clear visual hierarchy.

What You Will Learn:

  • Anatomy of Type: Discover the various parts that make up a letterform, such as serifs, ascenders, and descenders, and understand how they contribute to a typeface's character and legibility.
  • Type Classification: Learn to distinguish between different categories of typefaces, including serif, sans-serif, and display fonts, and understand their historical context and common applications.
  • Type Pairing & Hierarchy: Master the art of combining different typefaces to create visual interest and a clear hierarchy that guides the reader's eye through your design.
  • Readability & Legibility: Understand the crucial difference between these two concepts and learn how to optimize your typography for a comfortable and efficient reading experience.
  • Grid Systems: Explore the use of grids as a foundational tool for creating organized, consistent, and visually harmonious layouts.

By the end of this module, you will have the knowledge and skills to make informed typographic decisions that elevate your designs, enhance communication, and create a more engaging experience for your audience.
