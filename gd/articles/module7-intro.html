Image Making & Illustration

This module explores the diverse world of image making and illustration, essential skills for any graphic designer looking to create compelling and original visual content. We will delve into various techniques, from traditional hand-drawn methods to sophisticated digital workflows, and understand how to choose the right approach for different creative challenges.

What You Will Learn:

  • Hand-drawn vs. Digital: Compare and contrast the advantages and disadvantages of traditional and digital illustration techniques, and learn how to combine them for a hybrid approach.
  • Vector Illustration: Master the art of creating scalable and crisp graphics using vector-based software like Adobe Illustrator.
  • Photomanipulation: Discover the techniques for combining and altering photographs to create surreal, conceptual, or enhanced imagery.
  • Collage & Mixed Media: Explore the creative possibilities of combining different materials, textures, and images to create unique and expressive visual compositions.

By the end of this module, you will have a broader understanding of the various image-making techniques at your disposal, empowering you to create richer, more dynamic, and more engaging visual content for your design projects.
