Packaging & Print Design

This module delves into the specialized world of packaging and print design, where graphic design principles meet the tangible realities of physical production. Understanding the unique considerations for designing for physical products and print media is crucial for ensuring your designs translate effectively from screen to real-world applications.

What You Will Learn:

  • Die Lines: Understand the technical blueprints for creating custom-shaped packaging and printed materials.
  • CMYK vs. RGB: Grasp the fundamental differences between color models for print and digital, and how to ensure accurate color reproduction.
  • Finishing Techniques: Explore various post-printing processes that enhance the appearance, durability, and tactile quality of printed materials.
  • Eco-friendly Packaging: Learn about sustainable design practices and materials for minimizing environmental impact in packaging.

By the end of this module, you will have the knowledge to confidently design for print and packaging, ensuring your creations are not only visually stunning but also technically sound and ready for production.
