Layout and Composition

This module focuses on the principles of layout and composition, the art of arranging visual elements on a page to create a cohesive, aesthetically pleasing, and effective design. A strong layout not only looks good but also guides the viewer's eye, communicates a clear message, and enhances the overall user experience.

What You Will Learn:

  • Grids and Guides: Understand the importance of using grids and guides to create structured, organized, and consistent layouts.
  • White Space: Learn how to use negative space effectively to improve readability, create emphasis, and bring a sense of balance and sophistication to your designs.
  • The Golden Ratio and Rule of Thirds: Explore these classic compositional guidelines to create visually harmonious and dynamic layouts.
  • Modular Layouts: Discover the flexibility and scalability of modular design, a key concept in modern web and app design.

By the end of this module, you will have a solid understanding of how to create well-structured and visually compelling layouts that effectively communicate your message and engage your audience.
