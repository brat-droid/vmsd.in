Introduction to Graphic Design

This module serves as a foundational introduction to the vibrant and ever-evolving world of graphic design. We will explore its historical roots, understand its core principles, and discover its profound impact on our daily lives. By the end of this module, you will have a clear understanding of what graphic design is, what designers do, and the fundamental concepts that underpin all visual communication.

What You Will Learn:

  • The History of Graphic Design: Journey through the key movements and technological advancements that have shaped the field, from the invention of the printing press to the digital revolution.
  • Influential Design Movements: Dive into the philosophies and aesthetics of pivotal movements like the Bauhaus, the Swiss Style, and the Psychedelic Art movement, and understand their lasting influence.
  • The Role of a Graphic Designer Today: Explore the diverse and expanding roles of modern graphic designers, from branding and web design to UI/UX and motion graphics.

Whether you are a complete beginner or looking to solidify your foundational knowledge, this module will provide you with the essential context and inspiration to begin your journey into the art and science of graphic design.
