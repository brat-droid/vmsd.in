Motion Graphics

Motion graphics is a dynamic and rapidly expanding field within graphic design that involves bringing static designs to life through animation. It combines principles of graphic design, animation, and filmmaking to create compelling visual narratives for various media, including video, web, and interactive experiences. As digital platforms continue to dominate communication, the demand for skilled motion graphic designers has soared.

What You Will Learn:

  • Introduction to After Effects: Get acquainted with the industry-standard software for motion graphics, learning its interface, core concepts, and basic animation techniques.
  • Kinetic Typography: Discover the art of animating text to convey meaning and emotion, transforming static words into dynamic visual elements.
  • Logo Animation: Learn how to bring logos to life, adding movement and personality to brand identities.

By the end of this module, you will have a foundational understanding of motion graphics principles and the tools necessary to start creating your own animated designs, adding a powerful new dimension to your graphic design skill set.
