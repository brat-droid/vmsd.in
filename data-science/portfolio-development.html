Showcase Your Skills and Get Hired

A portfolio is a collection of your work that you can use to showcase your skills to potential employers. In this section, we will learn how to create a portfolio that will help you to get hired.

What to Include in Your Portfolio

Your portfolio should include a variety of projects that demonstrate your skills in data science. You should also include a resume and a cover letter. We will discuss what to include in each of these documents and provide some examples.

How to Create a Portfolio

There are a variety of ways to create a portfolio. You can use a website builder, such as Squarespace or Wix, or you can create your own website from scratch. We will discuss the pros and cons of each approach and help you to choose the right one for you.

Case Study: Creating a Portfolio for a Data Scientist

In this case study, we will create a portfolio for a data scientist. We will use a website builder to create a professional-looking portfolio that showcases the data scientist's skills and experience. We will also discuss how to promote your portfolio and get it in front of potential employers.
