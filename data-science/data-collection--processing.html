Collect and Process Your Data for Your Capstone Project

Once you have defined your problem statement, you need to collect and process your data. In this section, we will learn how to collect and process data for your capstone project.

Data Collection

There are a variety of ways to collect data, such as web scraping, APIs, and surveys. We will discuss the different data collection methods and help you to choose the right one for your project. We will also discuss the ethical considerations of data collection.

Data Processing

Once you have collected your data, you need to process it to get it ready for analysis. This includes tasks such as cleaning, transforming, and enriching your data. We will discuss the different data processing techniques and help you to choose the right ones for your project.

Case Study: Collecting and Processing Data for a Housing Price Prediction Project

In this case study, we will collect and process data for a housing price prediction project. We will use web scraping to collect data from a real estate website, and then we will use Pandas to clean and process the data. We will also discuss the challenges of working with real-world data and how to overcome them.
